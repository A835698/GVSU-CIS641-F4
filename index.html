Optimized Team Maker Project

Project Overview

Welcome to the heart of our project, the "Optimized Team Maker"! Our goal is to create a computer program that helps organizations put together the best teams for their projects or tasks. The Optimized Team Maker ensures that teams are made up of the right people with the right skills, fostering effective collaboration and goal achievement.
